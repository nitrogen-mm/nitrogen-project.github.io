Features

Status bar:

  • Advanced Setup battery (style, interest).
  • Date and time (font size, alignment, format, date).
  • Display 4 tiles in the row.
  • Enlarge the first row of tiles.
  • Turn the brightness slider.
  • Vibrate when you click on the tile.
  • Fast access.
  • System UI Tuner.
  • Counter Notification.
  • Weather in the status bar.
  • Indicator of network traffic.

Navigation bar:

  • Buttons and their location.
  • Height, width of the navigation bar.
  • Cursor to the left, to the right when typing.

Gestures:

  • Clicking on the status bar, navigation bar to sleep.
  • The gesture with three fingers to make a screenshot.

Lock screen:

  • Lock screen shortcuts.
  • Installing the wallpaper on the lock screen.
  • Weather on the lock screen.

Volume keys:

  • Waking up the volume buttons.
  • The player controls.
  • Answering a call button to increase the volume.

Other options:

  • Closing the application by holding down the back.
  • The sound of the camera shutter.
  • Force Maximized notice.
  • Messages in Immersive Mode.
  • Disable FC notice.
  • Enable search bar menu recently.
  • Dynamic color changes of the navigation bar.
  • Setting up DPI.
  • Rotate the screen.
  • Disable warning color in power saving mode.
  • Developer options are enabled by default.
  • Built SuperSU.
  • Initlogo.
  • Boot Logo.
  • Support BitSyko Layers.

Security:

  • Fast unlock after entering correct PIN code.
  • Stirring buttons PIN code.
